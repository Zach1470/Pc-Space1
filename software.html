Software

Software refers to the various programs and operating systems that run on a computer. It is the set of instructions that tells the computer what to do and how to do it.

There are many different types of software that are used for a variety of purposes, including productivity, entertainment, security, and more. Some common examples of software include operating systems such as Windows or macOS, office suites like Microsoft Office or Google Workspace, and media players like VLC or iTunes.

Software is typically installed on a computer through a process called installation, which involves downloading the software from the internet or purchasing in on a physical flash drive or dvd. Keeping the software on your computer up to date is important for ensuring that is performs well and is secure.
