RGB

RGB (Red Green Blue) is a type of lighting technology used in many computer systems and parts, including cases, keyboards, mice, and monitors. RGB lighting allows users to change the color and intensity of the light emitted by their computer system, often through the use of software or physical buttons on the device.

RGB lighting can be used to add a personalized touch to a computer system, or to create a specific mood or atmosphere. Many computer components with RGB lighting also has the ability to display a wide range of colors and patterns, including static colors, color-cycling, and even reactive lighting that changes in response to system activity or music.

In addition to aesthetics, RGB lighting can also be used to improve visibility in low-light environments, or to indicate the status of a system or device.
